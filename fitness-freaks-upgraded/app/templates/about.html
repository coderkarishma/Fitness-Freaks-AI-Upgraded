About FitnessFreak AI

Built to tackle two of the most common modern health problems — poor posture from desk work, and poor nutrition from busy schedules.

The Problem We Solve

Modern life is breaking our bodies.

Sedentary lifestyles, prolonged screen time, and poor dietary habits are contributing to musculoskeletal problems and metabolic disorders at record rates. FitnessFreak AI was created to give everyone — regardless of budget — access to intelligent, personalised health guidance.

We use computer vision (MediaPipe / TensorFlow.js) for real-time posture correction and nutritional science algorithms (Harris-Benedict equations) for diet planning.
